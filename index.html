About

When a fine dine reservation is at your own table, entrust us to make the meal unforgettable. From service, menu, chefs’ explanation of each course to sourcing the ingredients, the entire experience will be greater than that of dining at a fine dine restaurant. Not to forget, we will leave everything spotless

The Orange Apron is about discerning diners opting for a close gathering and treating their guests to an experience of a lifetime

How it works

The experience begins when you request a menu from us. Our Chefs send you their proposal and customize the menu and service according to your preference. On the day of the event, the Chef will buy the ingredients, cook them at your home, serve the meal and leave everything spotless - so you don’t have to worry about anything!

Personalize your own menu and have your Fine Dine Chef take care of the rest!

Fine Dining from your Kitchen

A few hours prior to your exclusive dining experience, your personal chef will arrive and begin preparing the menu agreed upon.

There’s an increasing eagerness among guests to learn about the food being served. Watch and learn authentic cooking techniques and culinary hacks from your chef as he cooks in your kitchen!

We'll be serving you each dish / Demystifying the plate

The chef walks you through every detail involved in each dish from the concept to ingredients so, you can have a unique private dining experience in the comfort of your own home.

The Chef will leave your kitchen spotless!

Cleaning up is an essential part of a spotless service! Before leaving, the chef and his team will make sure all the equipment and dinnerware involved in the service will be left as you found them.

Relax and enjoy the meal with peace of mind, knowing that the chef's gotten everything taken cared of!

The Head Chef

Chef Ephraim Ebenezer’s (Ebby) culinary experience began at the age of 5 in his mother’s kitchen. Motivated by the greats like Jacques Pepin and Paul Bocuse he pursued a career in the kitchen. A graduate of Hotel Management, he has an impressive culinary resume that has been influenced by a global palate and international experience. Ebby has worked in Dubai, Bangalore and Oman for the last 22years in Hyatt Regency, Dubai | Grand Hyatt, Oman & Shangri-La, Oman and specialises in Mediterranean (Italian, French & Spanish) cuisines and cooks Moroccan, Iranian & Lebanese having worked with award-winning kitchens.

You can compile your personal menu or choose from the menu. The menu is dedicated to the various outlets Ebby has worked for……
